18.02.2017

Cirque du Soleil's famouse show Kooza is in town! Watching them perform is like examining a collection of the most precious jem from the world. There're no word which can describe their majesty, just go if you have the chance. The name KOOZA is inspired by the Sanskrit word "koza", which means "box", "chest" or "treasure", and was chosen because one of the underlying concepts of the production is the idea of a "circus in a box".
